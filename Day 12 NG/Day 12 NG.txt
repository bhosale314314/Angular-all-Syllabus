
RxJS Operator:

1. from: Used to convert noramal array data into stream of data or observables.

2. of: Used to convert comma seperated data into stream of data or observables.

3. map: Map is used to covert data into new observables.

4. pipe: Can combine multiple operators at a single point. i.e. map, filter.

5. filter: Used to filter the data based on given condition.

Normal operators are unicast but special operators are multicast operators.

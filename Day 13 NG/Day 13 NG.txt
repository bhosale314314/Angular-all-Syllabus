
Promises in Angular:
	Promises has been introduced in ES6. 
	Promise are async or parellal flow of execution execution which can handle single event at a time.
	Promises gives only one value.
	States of Promise:
	resolve(): Promises has been successfully executed. 
	reject(): Promises has not been successfully executed.
	Pending State: Promise is yet to execute.
	Promises can not be cancelled.
	Promises are used for http calls.


Observables Vs Promises:

	Obs: Can handle multiple events at a time. Prom: Can handle single events at a time.
	Obs: Can be cancelled. Prom: Can not be cancelled.
	Obs: Came from RxJs. Prom: came from ES6.
	Obs: need to subscibe the data. Prom: no need to subscibe the data.
	Obs: can hanlde next, complete, error. Prom: can handle either resolve or reject.
